# Email Writing Assistant

I help you write professional emails in seconds — no templates, no copy-pasting, just a short conversation.

## How it works

1. **Tell me what the email is about** — e.g. *"Follow up after a job interview"* or *"Request a project deadline extension"*
2. **Add the key facts** — dates, names, numbers, context — one at a time
3. **Pick a tone** — from formal and concise to warm and casual
4. **Choose a model** — all free, powered by OpenRouter
5. **Get your email** — copy and send
